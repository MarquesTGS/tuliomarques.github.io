About

I am a Medical Physicist and MSc candidate at São Paulo State University (UNESP), with experience in diagnostic imaging, quality assurance, radiation protection, and artificial intelligence applied to medical imaging.

My research focuses on radiomics, machine learning, quantitative image analysis, and the development of innovative solutions for medical physics, including 3D-printed phantoms and automated quality control methodologies.
